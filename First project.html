PREPARE RICE WITH ME

This is the best procedure to prepare rice in your kitchen

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Salt
  • Water
  • Other spices if available

Instructions

  1. Take your sufuria and pour two cups of water
  2. Put one teaspoon of salt to the water
  3. Then add one cup of your rice
  4. Cover your sufuria and leave it to boil for 15 mins
